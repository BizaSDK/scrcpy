## 📋 What is scrcpy?

scrcpy is a modern program that helps you show and control your Android phone’s screen on your Windows PC. This app has a clean, easy-to-use window. It works without needing to type commands. With scrcpy, you can connect your device using a USB cable or over Wi-Fi. It automatically finds your phone and lets you adjust settings like screen quality and window size.

This application aims to make Android screen mirroring simple for everyone. Whether you want to make a presentation, play mobile games on a bigger screen, or check your phone faster, scrcpy does the job with no fuss.

### 6. Using scrcpy Wirelessly

To use scrcpy over Wi-Fi, follow these steps:

- Connect your phone via USB to your PC one last time.
- Open scrcpy and look for the option to enable wireless mode or open a command prompt window (if provided with the GUI) to start wireless debugging.
- The app will display your device’s IP address.
- Disconnect the USB cable.
- scrcpy should connect to the phone wirelessly and mirror the screen.

Note: Wired connections usually have more stable performance.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Tips

- If your phone is not detected, check the USB cable and port by trying a different cable or port.
- Ensure USB debugging is enabled on your phone.
- Restart your phone and PC if scrcpy does not start or connect.
- Disable any firewall or antivirus temporarily if they block the connection.
- Use the latest Android version supported or update your phone’s system.
- Try restarting the scrcpy app if the screen freezes.
- Check that both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network for wireless use.
